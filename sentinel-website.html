Sentinel sirf account nahi dekha. Wo dekha hai ki aap kis halat mein payment kar rahe ho. 12 behavioral signals, real time mein.

01
Signals Silently Collect Hote Hain
Jab aap payment shuru karte ho — background mein 12 behavioral signals collect hote hain. Copy-paste, recent calls, typing speed, confirm screen time. Koi UI change nahi. Aapko pata bhi nahi chalta.
02
BCD Engine Score Calculate Karta Hai
Behavioral Coercion Detection engine sab signals combine karta hai aur ek risk score banata hai. Single signal kabhi vault trigger nahi karta — sirf multiple strong signals milke karte hain.
